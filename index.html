About Me

Design-to-code specialist, accessibility advocate, RTL expert.

I'm a Senior Frontend Developer with over 10 years of experience converting Figma and Adobe XD designs into high-quality, responsive HTML interfaces. I bring strong expertise in SCSS, Git, and Pug, with hands-on experience in Angular, React, Vue, and Next.js using TypeScript and Tailwind CSS.

I build data-rich dashboards with Highcharts, ApexCharts, amCharts, Recharts, and D3, and engineered a reusable high-performance front-end pipeline (Pug, SCSS, Webpack, critical-CSS inlining, PurgeCSS) used across 40+ client deliverables — including Drupal and WordPress theming, and Arabic-first PDF, ticket, and responsive email templates.

My specialty is Arabic (RTL) websites and MADA accessibility compliance, delivering government and enterprise platforms in Qatar that are inclusive, performant, and beautiful in both directions — shipped through Docker and Azure DevOps CI/CD pipelines.
